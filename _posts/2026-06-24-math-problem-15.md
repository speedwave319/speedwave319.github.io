## Question 1
Let $x > 0$.

(a) Find $\displaystyle \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{x + h} - \sqrt{x}}{h}$.

(b) Hence, find $\displaystyle \frac{d}{dx} e^{\sqrt{x}}$ from first principles.

($2 + 3$ marks)
