5. (a) Prove, by mathematical induction, that

$\cos x + \cos 2x + \cdots + \cos(nx) = \frac{\sin\!\left(\left(n+\tfrac12\right)x\right)}{2\sin\!\left(\tfrac{x}{2}\right)} -\frac12$, for all positive integers $n$, where $\sin\!\left(\tfrac{x}{2}\right) \neq 0$.



(b) Hence, or otherwise, evaluate

$$
\lim_{x \to 0}
\frac{\sin\!\left(\left(n+\tfrac12\right)x\right)}
{x \,\sin\!\left(\tfrac{x}{2}\right)},
\qquad \text{where } n \text{ is a positive integer}.
$$

($6 + 2$ marks)
